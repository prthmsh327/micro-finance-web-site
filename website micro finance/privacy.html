Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Your Data Stays in Your Browser

RupeeCalc is a static website. Every calculation — EMI, SIP, salary, tax — runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. We never transmit your financial data to any server. We never store it. We never see it.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand aggregate traffic (page views, device types, general location). This data is anonymized and used only to improve the site. We do not use it to identify individual users.

Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising at google.com/settings/ads.

Cookies

RupeeCalc itself sets no cookies. Google Analytics and AdSense may set their own cookies subject to Google's privacy policy.

Disclaimer

All calculators on RupeeCalc are for informational and educational purposes only. They do not constitute financial, tax, or investment advice. Always verify important financial decisions with a qualified professional.
